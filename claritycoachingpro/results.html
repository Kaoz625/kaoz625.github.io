Career Pivot
Before

Marketing Director at a mid-size agency. Fourteen-hour days, two promotions in three years, and a growing sense that she was excellent at a job she no longer wanted.

The Work

Six-month engagement. Clarified values and career drivers. Identified a transferable skills profile. Built a transition strategy that reduced financial risk while testing new directions.

After

Sarah helped me realize I wasn't burned out from working too hard — I was burned out from working on the wrong things. That distinction changed everything.

Founder Clarity

The Work

VIP Day followed by monthly coaching. Worked through leadership identity, decision-making frameworks, and the co-founder dynamic. Built a structure for difficult conversations.

Entrepreneur Stuck
Before

E-commerce entrepreneur, 36. Business was profitable but had plateaued. She felt stuck in operations, unable to think strategically, and increasingly resentful of a business she'd built from nothing.

The Work

Monthly coaching for 7 months. Identified the role she needed to step out of and the leader she needed to hire. Worked on delegation, identity as a CEO, and rebuilding her relationship with the business.

After

Hired an operations lead, stepped into a true CEO role. Revenue grew 40% in 10 months. Says she "finally likes coming to work again" — and that she didn't realize how much she'd missed that.
