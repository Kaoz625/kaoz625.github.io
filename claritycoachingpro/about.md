Meet Your Coach

Dr. Sarah Chen,\
*PCC*
================

I grew up in Queens as a first-generation college student, driven by a relentless need to prove something. I got into Columbia, earned a master's in psychology, and then spent eight years in corporate finance — performing well, moving up, and quietly falling apart.

Burnout has a way of making things clear. I left the corporate world in 2015 with a PCC certification from ICF and a single conviction: that most high-achievers are solving the wrong problem. They're optimizing execution when what they need is clarity about direction.

Since then, I've worked with over 200 clients — executives at Fortune 500 companies, early-stage founders, people at career crossroads, and anyone who feels the gap between where they are and where they know they could be. The work is rigorous, practical, and it produces results that last.

My Approach

</div>

## Clarity Over Comfort.

The coaching philosophy at Clarity is grounded in three commitments that shape every session, every conversation, and every recommendation.

</div>

<div class="philosophy-grid">

<div class="philosophy-card">

### Clarity over comfort

Good coaching isn't always comfortable. I'll ask the questions that matter, not just the easy ones. The goal isn't to feel better in the session — it's to make decisions you won't second-guess a year from now.

</div>

<div class="philosophy-card">

### Evidence over intuition alone

I use validated frameworks from positive psychology, cognitive behavioral theory, and leadership research. Feelings matter enormously — and they're most useful when paired with structured thinking tools that help you act on them.

</div>

<div class="philosophy-card">

### Progress over perfection

The clients who transform most rapidly aren't the ones who plan the most carefully. They're the ones willing to move before everything feels perfectly aligned. We build momentum by starting imperfectly and adjusting fast.

</div>
