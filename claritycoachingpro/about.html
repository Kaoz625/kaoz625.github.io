My Approach

Clarity Over Comfort.

The coaching philosophy at Clarity is grounded in three commitments that shape every session, every conversation, and every recommendation.

Clarity over comfort

Good coaching isn't always comfortable. I'll ask the questions that matter, not just the easy ones. The goal isn't to feel better in the session — it's to make decisions you won't second-guess a year from now.

Evidence over intuition alone

I use validated frameworks from positive psychology, cognitive behavioral theory, and leadership research. Feelings matter enormously — and they're most useful when paired with structured thinking tools that help you act on them.

Progress over perfection

The clients who transform most rapidly aren't the ones who plan the most carefully. They're the ones willing to move before everything feels perfectly aligned. We build momentum by starting imperfectly and adjusting fast.
