Our Story

</div>

<div class="section-title">

Started in a Brooklyn\
Garage in 2014

</div>

**Mike Vargas and James Okafor** grew up in East Flatbush and Bed-Stuy. Both trained as HVAC techs in their early twenties, working for large contractors who cared more about upsells than actual repairs. They watched the same story play out over and over: a homeowner calls for a repair, gets a sky-high quote, and either pays it or goes without heat in January.

In 2014, they pooled their savings, bought a used van, and launched Peak Pro out of Mike's garage on Atlantic Ave. The rules were simple: **flat-rate pricing, licensed techs only, and no job left half-done**. Word spread fast in Brooklyn the way it always does — one neighbor telling another.

Today we run a team of eight certified technicians, serve all five boroughs, and still operate by the same rules we set in that garage. Mike handles operations. James runs the field crew. The van number went from one to seven. The prices stayed honest.

What Makes Us Different

</div>

<div class="diff-grid">

<div class="diff-item">

### 💰 Flat-Rate Pricing

You get a firm price before we touch anything. The quote is the bill. We don't charge by the hour so there's no incentive to drag out the job.

</div>

<div class="diff-item">

### ⚡ 1-Hour Emergency Response

For Brooklyn calls, we aim to be on-site within 60 minutes. For the rest of NYC, 90 minutes. We answer our emergency line on the first ring — always.

</div>

<div class="diff-item">

### 🚫 No Hidden Fees

No fuel surcharges, no "after hours" markup on labor, no material padding. What we quote is what you pay. Period.

</div>

<div class="diff-item">

### 👷 Licensed Techs Only

Every person we send to your home is a licensed, background-checked employee. We never subcontract jobs out to whoever is cheapest that day.

</div>

<div class="diff-item">

### 🔁 Work Guaranteed

Every repair carries a 90-day labor guarantee. Every installation carries a full manufacturer warranty plus our own 1-year workmanship guarantee.

</div>

<div class="diff-item">

### 📍 Real Brooklyn People

We live here. Our kids go to school here. When we fix your heat, we're fixing a neighbor's heat. That changes how you work.
