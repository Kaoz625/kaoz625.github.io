Who We Are

Veterans. Dogs. Community. Tech.

NYC Tailblazers is a veteran-focused community organization operating in New York City. We were founded on a simple belief: veterans who served this country deserve community, resources, and opportunity — and the city's dogs deserve proper spaces to run free.

We operate at the intersection of veteran advocacy, community organizing, and technology. Our team builds AI tools, websites, and digital services — with proceeds funding our mission programs.

The Gentleman Brand Management website you are viewing is one of our service projects. We build professional websites for veteran-owned and community-focused businesses to generate sustainable funding for our work.

Our Three Pillars

🐶
Dog Park Advocacy
Advocating for better, safer, and more accessible dog parks across all five NYC boroughs. Because dogs and their owners deserve great spaces.
⛳
Veteran Community
Connecting veterans with resources, community, and each other. Veterans helping veterans — the same mission-first mentality that defines service.
💻
Tech for Good
Building AI tools, websites, and digital infrastructure that generates revenue for our mission while serving community organizations and veteran-owned businesses.
