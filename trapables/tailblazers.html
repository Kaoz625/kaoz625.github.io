Behind the Brand

NYC Tailblazers

The NYC-based team that builds brands, digital storefronts, and online presence for independent operators doing things their own way.

Who We Are

NYC Tailblazers is a small independent agency based in New York City. We build websites, brands, and digital tools for operators who don't fit the corporate mold — street brands, independent businesses, and anyone building something real.

We built Trapables from the ground up — brand identity, multi-page PWA storefront, cinematic animations, split-payment tooling, and full strain archive. We handle the digital layer so operators can focus on the product.

What We Build

  • ✓PWA-first storefronts (static, no monthly fees, mobile-native)
  • ✓Brand identity — logo, palette, typography, voice
  • ✓Custom ordering flows and payment tooling
  • ✓Social media setup and content strategy
  • ✓Cloudflare, GitHub Pages, Coolify deployments — free hosting
