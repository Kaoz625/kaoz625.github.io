Lead Colorist & Owner
Sofia Reyes
Lead Colorist & Owner · 12 Years Experience
12Years in the industry

Sofia founded Luxe & Co. in 2018 with a single conviction: that Williamsburg deserved a color studio built on artistry, not volume. Trained at the Sassoon Academy in New York and further refined in London, her approach to color is architectural — every decision is deliberate, every tone is earned. She specializes in balayage, color correction, and the kind of lived-in blondes that look effortless but take genuine expertise to execute. New clients are her favorite: she loves a challenge, and she especially loves the moment someone sees themselves differently for the first time.

Specialties

Balayage Color Correction Lived-In Blondes Highlights & Lowlights Bridal Color

Daniela Park
Extension Specialist & Senior Stylist · 8 Years Experience
8Years in the industry

Daniela has built her reputation on one promise: extensions that no one can detect. She is certified in both hand-tied and tape-in methods, and her eye for color-matching is nothing short of extraordinary. Beyond extensions, she brings the same precision to bridal styling — she has been the hands behind over 80 weddings and counting. Her approach is methodical and thorough: she wants to know your life, your routine, and your hair goals before she picks up a single weft. If you've been hesitant about extensions, Daniela is who will change your mind.

Specialties

Hand-Tied Extensions Tape-In Extensions Bridal Hair Precision Cuts Bridal Updo

Jordan Vasquez
Color Technician & Junior Stylist · 4 Years Experience
4Years in the industry

Jordan came up under Sofia's mentorship and has become the studio's resident authority on vivid, unconventional color. If you've ever wanted to try something bold but didn't know who to trust with it — Jordan is your person. They have an instinct for which vivid tones will genuinely flatter a face, and they execute color-to-color transitions with a technical confidence that belies their years. Beyond vivid work, Jordan is the studio's go-to for men's cuts and a wide range of treatment services. Energy is warm, vibes are immaculate, and the chair is always comfortable.
